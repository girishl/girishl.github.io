About Me

I am presently serving as Director, Training and Placement and Head of the Department of AI&DS at Shridevi Institute of Engineering and Technology in Tumkur. With 13+ years of experience in teaching and research, I am passionate about nurturing talent and driving innovation.

My expertise spans Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, DevOps, and AI. I actively mentor students through AIMinds and Devoid clubs, organize TensorFlow User Group (TFUG) in Tumkur, and contribute to open-source projects.

As a Thesis Supervisor at Liverpool John Moores University, I bring an international dimension to my academic contributions, fostering collaborative and community-driven technological advancements.
